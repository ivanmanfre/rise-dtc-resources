RISE DTC · Founder Video

Your thank-you video, in one take.

Record this once. It plays on every page a new brand owner lands on after they reach out. Warm, founder-to-camera, 60–90 seconds.

⏱ 60–90 seconds 🎥 Phone or webcam, eye level 💡 Window light in front of you 🔁 3 takes, keep the warmest
How to read this: The green “SAY THIS” blocks are word-for-word — say those close to verbatim so the open and close land. Everything in the white boxes is talking points: say them your way, into the lens, like you’re talking to one person. Don’t read those flat. The only middle part to get precise is the model, and those are your own site’s words.
Beat 1

Open ~10s

“Hey, it’s Mattan, founder of RISE DTC. First off, thank you. You just did the one thing most brand owners never get around to, so you’re already ahead of the pack. Quick minute on who we are and what happens next.”

Smile, relaxed, like you’re glad they showed up. This line has to feel human — don’t rush it.

Beat 2

Who we are your words · ~20s

Riff on these

  • You’ve run Meta ads for 20+ years, 150+ DTC brands, $725M in attributed revenue.
  • RISE exists because you got tired of watching agencies get paid whether the brand grew or not.
  • You built the thing you wished existed when you were in their seat.

Personal, not a stat dump. Pick one or two numbers you actually feel and say them like they mean something.

Beat 3

The model the differentiator · say it precisely

“We’re the world’s first performance-only growth partner for DTC brands. No retainers, no percentage of ad spend, no creative fees. We only earn when we generate measurable growth for your brand. If you don’t win, we don’t win.”

Then one line, your voice

  • Every other agency bills you whether the number moves or not. You wanted skin in the game, so you put it there.
Beat 4

What happens next your words · ~15s

Keep it evergreen — works on any page

  • “If we’ve got a call booked, come with your real numbers — ad spend, revenue, where you feel stuck. I’ll tell you straight whether we can move them.”
  • “If you grabbed a resource, it’s in your inbox — use it, and reach out when you want a second set of eyes on the account.”

Say the version that fits, or say both loosely. Don’t overthink which page it’s on.

Beat 5

Close ~8s

“Either way, I’m glad you’re here. Let’s go find the growth that’s already sitting in your account. Talk soon.”

End on the smile. Don’t add a tagline after this — let it land and stop.

A few things before you hit record

  • Tone: you’re talking to one founder over coffee, not presenting. Warmth beats polish on these.
  • Don’t: read it robotically, sit too far back, or add a hard pitch. The video builds trust; the call does the selling.
  • If it runs long: cut Beat 2 down. Never cut the model or the open/close.
  • Optional: record a second 8-second tail that’s call-specific (“looking forward to our call, one ask before we talk…”) and we’ll swap it onto the booked-call page only. Not needed for v1.

Model crib — your own site’s words

If you blank on Beat 3, these are the lines to hit:

  • “world’s first Performance-Only Growth Partner for DTC Brands”
  • “NO retainers, NO percentage of ad spend, NO creative fees”
  • “We only earn when we generate measurable growth. If you don’t win, neither do we.”
